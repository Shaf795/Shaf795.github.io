Academic Background

I am a Mechanical Engineering researcher with a PhD specializing in Linear Elastic Fracture Mechanics (LEFM) from the Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad, India. My academic training is rooted in solid mechanics, stress analysis, and numerical methods for structural integrity assessment.

My work combines theoretical understanding with computational modeling, with particular emphasis on finite element analysis of cracked components under complex loading conditions.

Research Focus

My primary research interests lie in fracture mechanics, mixed-mode crack behavior, stress intensity factor evaluation, and the application of finite element methods to solve engineering fracture problems.

I have experience working with industry-standard simulation tools such as ANSYS APDL and ABAQUS (with Python scripting) for parametric studies, automation, and post-processing of fracture mechanics simulations.

In addition to numerical and theoretical methods, I have experience with experimental stress analysis techniques, particularly photoelasticity. This includes understanding the principles of stress-induced birefringence and the use of circular polariscope setups to visualize and interpret stress distributions and stress concentration effects.

Teaching & Mentorship Philosophy

I believe that effective teaching in mechanical engineering must emphasize physical interpretation alongside mathematical formulation. My approach focuses on helping students understand the underlying mechanics behind equations, simulation results, and failure behavior.

Through this platform, I aim to support undergraduate students, postgraduate learners, and PhD scholars by providing structured explanations, simulation insights, and practical guidance for research-oriented problem solving.

Professional Outlook

This website is developed as a long-term academic resource and professional profile. It reflects my interest in teaching, research collaboration, and contributing to engineering practice through computational mechanics and structural integrity analysis.
